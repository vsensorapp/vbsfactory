Advanced Analysis

EMC, Power distribution

VBS uses the latest technology in power distribution analysis for ensuring there are no signal and power bottlenecks that may cause system power failure.
EMC simulation ensures compliance to FCC ordinances.

PCB Mass Production

Stencil design, panel design, fabrication

Our PCBs are designed for mass production. All CNC coding are done by us for multi layer PCBs.
The matching production fixtures are designed by us.

Mechanical component production

Plastic mold design, sheet metal die design, fabrication

We provide plastic injection mold design and production services as well as sheet metal mass production using metal die.
All molds are made using precision CNC.

Assembly - Manual and Automated

Autoplacers, manual solderers, functional testing

Assembly is conducted using machine feeders or manual soldering.
100% functional testing services after assembly can be provided.

High Integrity Software
